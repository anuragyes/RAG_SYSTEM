# Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

## Overview
MGNREGA is a demand-driven wage employment programme that guarantees 100 days of unskilled manual work per year to every rural household whose adult members volunteer to do so. It is the world's largest work guarantee programme.

## Eligibility Criteria
- Must be a resident of rural India
- Must be above 18 years of age
- Must be willing to do unskilled manual work
- Must be a member of a rural household (household = nuclear family)
- Must apply for a Job Card at the local Gram Panchayat
- No income limit — all rural adults are eligible

## Benefits
- **100 days of guaranteed employment** per household per year
- **Wage rate**: State-specific notified wage rate (₹200-350 per day depending on state)
- **Unemployment allowance**: If work not provided within 15 days, entitled to unemployment allowance (1/4th to 1/2 of wage rate)
- **Work within 5 km**: Employment provided within 5 km radius of residence
- **Worksite facilities**: Drinking water, shade, first aid at worksite
- **Equal wages**: Men and women receive equal wages
- **Childcare**: If 5+ children below 6 years at worksite, one worker deputed for childcare

## Types of Work
- Water conservation and water harvesting
- Drought proofing (afforestation/tree planting)
- Irrigation canals
- Renovation of traditional water bodies
- Land development
- Flood control and protection
- Rural connectivity (roads)
- Any other work notified by the Central Government

## How to Apply
1. Visit your Gram Panchayat office
2. Apply for a Job Card (free of cost)
3. Job Card issued within 15 days with photograph
4. Submit written application for work demand
5. Dated receipt must be given for application

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar Card (for identity)
- Address proof (rural residence)
- Passport-size photograph
- Bank/Post Office account details (for wage payment through DBT)
