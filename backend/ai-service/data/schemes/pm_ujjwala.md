# PM Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) 2.0

## Overview
Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana provides free LPG connections to women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) households. PMUY 2.0 was launched in August 2021 with simplified enrollment and no documentation requirements for migrants.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Target Group**: Women belonging to BPL families
- **Age**: Applicant must be a woman above 18 years of age
- **No existing LPG connection**: The household should not have an existing LPG connection
- **PMUY 2.0 expansion**: Includes migrant workers' families — no ration card or address proof needed for migrants
- **Priority Categories**:
  - SC/ST households
  - Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) beneficiaries
  - Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) beneficiaries
  - Forest dwellers
  - Most Backward Classes (MBC)
  - Tea and ex-tea garden tribes
  - People residing in islands and river islands

## Benefits
- **Free LPG connection** (worth approximately ₹1,600)
- **Free first refill** and **free hotplate** (stove) under PMUY 2.0
- **Subsidy on refills**: EMI facility for purchasing refills
- **No security deposit**: Security deposit for cylinder and pressure regulator borne by government

## How to Apply
1. Visit nearest LPG distributor (HP, Bharat, Indane)
2. Apply online at pmuy.gov.in
3. Apply through Common Service Centre (CSC)
4. Contact 1800-266-6696 (toll-free helpline)

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar Card (woman applicant)
- BPL certificate/Ration Card
- Bank account details (for subsidy transfer)
- Passport-size photograph
- For PMUY 2.0 migrants: Self-declaration of address is sufficient
