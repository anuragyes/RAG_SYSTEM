# PM Scholarship Scheme (PMSS)

## Overview
Scholarships for wards of ex-servicemen and CAPF personnel for professional education.

## Eligibility
- Wards/widows of Ex-Servicemen, Coast Guard, CAPF, Assam Rifles
- Minimum 60% in qualifying exam
- First professional degree (Engineering, MBBS, MBA, etc.)
- Age below 25 (graduation), 27 (post-graduation)

## Benefits
- Boys: ₹2,500/month (₹30,000/year)
- Girls: ₹3,000/month (₹36,000/year)
- For full course duration

## How to Apply
1. Apply at ksb.gov.in
2. Window: October-November yearly

## Documents
- Discharge certificate, Mark sheets, Aadhaar, Bank account

## Ministry
Ministry of Defence / Ministry of Home Affairs
