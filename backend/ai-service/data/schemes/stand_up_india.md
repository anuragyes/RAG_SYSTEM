# Stand Up India Scheme

## Overview
Stand Up India facilitates bank loans between ₹10 Lakh and ₹1 Crore to at least one SC/ST borrower and one Woman borrower per bank branch for setting up a greenfield enterprise in manufacturing, services, agri-allied activities, or trading.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Target Group**: SC/ST and/or Women Entrepreneurs
- **Age**: 18 years and above
- **Enterprise Type**: Greenfield enterprise only (first-time venture)
- **Loan Purpose**: Setting up new enterprise in:
  - Manufacturing sector
  - Services sector
  - Agri-allied activities
  - Trading sector
- **Borrower should NOT be in default** to any bank/financial institution
- **In case of non-individual enterprises**: At least 51% shareholding and controlling stake must be held by SC/ST or Woman entrepreneur

## Benefits
- **Loan Amount**: ₹10 Lakh to ₹1 Crore
- **Composite loan**: Covers both term loan and working capital
- **Repayment Period**: Up to 7 years
- **Moratorium Period**: Maximum 18 months
- **Margin Money**: Up to 25% (can be provided through convergence with eligible Central/State schemes)
- **Rate of Interest**: Lowest applicable rate of the bank (base rate/MCLR + 3% + tenor premium)
- Available at all scheduled commercial bank branches

## How to Apply
1. Visit standupmitra.in portal
2. Approach the nearest bank branch directly
3. Apply through SIDBI's Stand Up Connect centres
4. Contact District Lead Bank Manager

## Required Documents
- Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID)
- Caste certificate (for SC/ST applicants)
- Address proof
- Business plan/Project report
- Quotation of machinery/equipment
- Proof of business premises (owned/rented)
- Last 6 months' bank statements
- IT returns (if available)
- Partnership deed/MOA (if applicable)
