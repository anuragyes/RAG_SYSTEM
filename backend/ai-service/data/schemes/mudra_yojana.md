# Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY)

## Overview
MUDRA provides loans up to ₹10 Lakh to non-corporate, non-farm small/micro enterprises for income-generating activities.

## Loan Categories
- **Shishu**: Up to ₹50,000 (for startups)
- **Kishore**: ₹50,001 to ₹5 Lakh (for growth stage)
- **Tarun**: ₹5,00,001 to ₹10 Lakh (for established businesses)

## Eligibility
- Any Indian citizen with a business plan for non-farm income generating activity
- Manufacturing, trading, services, allied agriculture activities
- Small shopkeepers, vendors, artisans, food service units, repair shops
- No collateral required for loans up to ₹10 Lakh

## Benefits
- No collateral/security needed
- No processing fee
- Repayment period: 5-7 years
- Available at all banks, NBFCs, MFIs

## How to Apply
1. Visit any bank branch or mudra.org.in
2. Apply through Udyamimitra portal

## Documents
- Identity proof, Address proof, Business plan, Photos

## Ministry
Ministry of Finance
