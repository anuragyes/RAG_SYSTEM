# Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

## Overview
Atal Pension Yojana is a government-backed pension scheme targeted at the unorganised sector workers. It provides a guaranteed minimum pension of ₹1,000 to ₹5,000 per month after the age of 60 years, depending on the contribution amount.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Age**: 18 to 40 years (entry age)
- **Citizenship**: Must be an Indian citizen
- **Bank Account**: Must have a savings bank account
- **Aadhaar**: Must have Aadhaar number
- **Not a member** of any statutory social security scheme (EPF, EPS, ESIC etc.)
- **Not an income tax payer** (for availing government co-contribution - applicable for applications before March 2016)
- **Sector**: Primarily for unorganised sector workers

## Pension Amounts & Monthly Contributions
| Monthly Pension | Contribution (Age 18) | Contribution (Age 25) | Contribution (Age 35) |
|---|---|---|---|
| ₹1,000/month | ₹42 | ₹76 | ₹181 |
| ₹2,000/month | ₹84 | ₹151 | ₹362 |
| ₹3,000/month | ₹126 | ₹226 | ₹543 |
| ₹4,000/month | ₹168 | ₹301 | ₹722 |
| ₹5,000/month | ₹210 | ₹376 | ₹902 |

## Benefits
- **Guaranteed pension**: ₹1,000 to ₹5,000 per month after 60 years
- **Spouse pension**: Same pension amount to spouse after subscriber's death
- **Nominee benefit**: Accumulated pension wealth returned to nominee after both deaths
- **Government co-contribution**: 50% of total contribution or ₹1,000/year (whichever is lower) for 5 years — for subscribers who joined before 31 March 2016
- **Tax benefit**: Contributions eligible for deduction under Section 80CCD(1)

## How to Apply
1. Visit any bank where you have a savings account
2. Fill APY registration form
3. Provide Aadhaar and mobile number
4. Online through net banking of major banks
5. Through NSDL/CRA APY portal

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar Card
- Bank account (savings)
- Mobile number linked to bank account
- Nominee details

## Ministry
Ministry of Finance (administered by PFRDA - Pension Fund Regulatory & Development Authority)
