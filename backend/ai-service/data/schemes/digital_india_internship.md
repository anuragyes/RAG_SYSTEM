# Digital India Internship Scheme

## Overview
Provides internship opportunities to students in various government ministries and departments under Digital India initiative.

## Eligibility
- Indian students pursuing B.Tech/BE, MCA, M.Tech, MBA (IT) or equivalent
- Minimum 60% marks in ongoing course
- Age: 18-25 years
- Must not have completed any government internship before

## Benefits
- ₹10,000/month stipend
- Duration: 2-6 months
- Certificate from Government of India
- Exposure to e-governance projects

## How to Apply
1. Apply at digitalindia.gov.in/internship
2. Applications open twice a year (Jan & July)

## Documents
- College ID, Mark sheets, Aadhaar, Resume

## Ministry
Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology
