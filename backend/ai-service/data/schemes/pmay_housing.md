# Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) - Housing for All

## Overview
PMAY aims to provide affordable housing to the urban and rural poor by 2024 (extended). The scheme has two components: PMAY-Urban (PMAY-U) and PMAY-Gramin (PMAY-G).

## PMAY-Urban

### Eligibility Criteria
- **EWS (Economically Weaker Section)**: Annual household income up to ₹3 Lakh
- **LIG (Low Income Group)**: Annual household income between ₹3-6 Lakh
- **MIG-I (Middle Income Group I)**: Annual household income between ₹6-12 Lakh
- **MIG-II (Middle Income Group II)**: Annual household income between ₹12-18 Lakh
- Must NOT own a pucca house anywhere in India
- Women ownership/co-ownership mandatory for EWS and LIG

### Benefits
- **EWS/LIG**: Subsidy of ₹2.67 Lakh on home loans up to ₹6 Lakh at 6.5% for 20 years
- **MIG-I**: Subsidy of ₹2.35 Lakh on home loans up to ₹9 Lakh at 4% for 20 years
- **MIG-II**: Subsidy of ₹2.30 Lakh on home loans up to ₹12 Lakh at 3% for 20 years
- Direct financial assistance of ₹1.5 Lakh for EWS in-situ rehabilitation

## PMAY-Gramin (Rural)

### Eligibility Criteria
- Must be from rural area
- Must NOT own a pucca house
- Selected from SECC 2011 data after Gram Sabha verification
- Priority to: houseless, those living in 0,1,2 room kutcha houses
- Priority groups: SC/ST, Minorities, freed bonded labourers, PwD, transgender, widows, ex-servicemen

### Benefits
- ₹1.20 Lakh in plain areas, ₹1.30 Lakh in hilly/difficult/IAP areas
- Additionally eligible for 90/95 person-days of unskilled labour from MGNREGA
- Support for toilet construction (₹12,000 from SBM-G)

## How to Apply
### Urban
1. Visit pmaymis.gov.in
2. Apply through Urban Local Body (ULB)
3. Apply through Common Service Centre (CSC)

### Rural
1. Apply through Gram Panchayat
2. Visit pmayg.nic.in

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar Card
- Income certificate
- Proof of no existing pucca house
- Bank account details
- Caste certificate (if applicable)

## Ministry
Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs (PMAY-U)
Ministry of Rural Development (PMAY-G)
