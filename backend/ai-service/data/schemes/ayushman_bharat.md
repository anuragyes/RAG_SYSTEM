# Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY)

## Overview
Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY is the world's largest health insurance/assurance scheme fully financed by the government. It provides a health cover of ₹5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization to over 12 crore poor and vulnerable families.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Target Group**: Bottom 40% of the Indian population (approx. 12 crore families)
- **Identification**: Based on deprivation and occupational criteria of SECC 2011 data
- **Rural Eligibility** (any one):
  - Households with no adult member between age 16-59
  - Female-headed household with no adult male member between 16-59
  - Household with differently-abled member and no able-bodied adult member
  - SC/ST households
  - Landless households deriving major part of income from manual casual labour
  - Households without shelter, destitute, living on alms
  - Manual scavenger families
  - Primitive tribal groups
  - Legally released bonded labour
- **Urban Eligibility** (based on occupation):
  - Rag picker, Beggar, Domestic worker
  - Street vendor, Cobbler, Hawker
  - Construction worker, Plumber, Mason, Painter
  - Sweeper, Sanitation worker, Mali
  - Home-based artisans, Handicraft workers
  - Transport workers (rickshaw puller, auto/taxi driver, etc.)
  - Shop workers, Assistants, Peons, Helpers, Delivery assistants
  - Electricians, Mechanics, Assemblers, Repair workers
  - Washerman, Chowkidar, Security guard

## Benefits
- ₹5 Lakh health coverage per family per year
- Covers 1,929 medical/surgical packages including:
  - Surgery, Medical and Day care treatments
  - Medicine, Diagnostics, Transport
  - Pre and post-hospitalization expenses (up to 15 days)
- Cashless and paperless treatment at empanelled hospitals
- No cap on family size or age
- Pre-existing conditions covered from Day 1

## How to Apply
1. Check eligibility at mera.pmjay.gov.in
2. Visit nearest Ayushman Bharat Mitra at empanelled hospital
3. Call 14555 (toll-free helpline)
4. Visit Common Service Centre (CSC)

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar Card or Ration Card
- SECC database reference
- Any government-issued ID
